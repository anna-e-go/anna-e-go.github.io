Camp Kesem

To put it simply, Kesem has my whole heart.
Camp Kesem is a national non-profit run by college students that supports children through and beyond a parent's cancer.

What exactly does that mean? Duke University's chapter provides a free week of summer camp to local children affected by a parent's cancer. Seeing campers transform into the most confident and compassionate versions of themselves is truly magical, and Camp Kesem is one of the best organizations I've been a part of.
